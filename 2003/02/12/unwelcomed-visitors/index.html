Unwelcomed Visitors

Posted byEmily February 12, 2003 Leave a comment on Unwelcomed Visitors

Them: It’s X and Y from downstairs.
Me: (Er, okay?!) Hold on… (rush to get Eunie to open door)

E: Hi?
Them: Hi, we live under you and we’d wanted to come by all term to tell you this, but could you please quiet down? We hear yelling, and running around and our whole apartment shakes, and do you guys move like furniture or something? We can’t sleep because it bothers us til like 3 AM.
E: Really? Where does it come from?
One of them: I live in the room beside the bathroom.
E: Oh, okay, I’ll tell my housemate. Sorry about that. (closes door)

E: (to me) Yo, you better pipe down “housemate”. Stop YELLING.
(We erupt in laughter).
Me: What do we move? (proceed to open and close my drawers, turn on my music, and move my chair) And yelling? G and I must get into fights and run around the small area that we do have playing cat and mouse…tho’ I suppose my room is more echoey than hers since it’s bigger and has less furniture to absorb sound. Tho’, I have lived here 4 years and no one has yet complained nor have I complained because of the cheap paper thin walls/ceilings/floors that we live and put up with daily in exchange for cheap rent. (Last term, DD asked me if the guy upstairs was bowling, it was quite amusing). I can hear the guy peeing upstairs loud and clear (only about 4 times a day–I guess he doesn’t drink enuff fluids)…maybe we should go upstairs and complain that he pees too loudly?
E: (idea!) Hey, you got a bucket? Let’s collectively go to the can together…
(More laughter)
(Soon, I get bored, and return to my work)

10 mins later…
*Knock, knock*..pause..knocks louder
(D’oh! Eunie is out, I put on a T and answer the door).
Me: Hi?
Them: Hi, we were just here, and we talked to your housemate 5 mins ago.
Me: Oh? There’s no one home but me. I was in my room studying. What’s up?
Them: Are you the one that lives next to the bathroom?
Me: Yes??
Them: We hear a lot of noise coming from your apartment til like 3 AM all the time. Tho’ maybe it isn’t your apartment (but after much investigation, it appparently is)..and from your room.
Me: Noise? I was just studying…and I’m usually asleep by 3 AM…(I sleep around 2-2h30ish)
Them: Were you playing music just now? And we heard parts of conversations…sounds like you were yelling.
Me: Yelling? There’s no one here…most of the time no one is home. I just study…tho’ I was playing music..(pauses)..can you hear?
Them: (Strains to hear the music that is playing softly in my room). Yeah, actually, it’s not the music that bothers us…it’s more like furniture moving. Do you move furniture around a lot?
Me: Furniture?!
Them: Chair maybe?
Me: Oh yeah, well, I do sit on a chair and I get up every so often..that may be it, since it has no wheels so it may scrape the floor.
Them: Doesn’t your room have carpet? (to absorb any sound)
Me: (I give them an incredulous look as if they are from outer space) Nooo….
Them: Oh. Well, um, it greatly bothers us. Would you try to keep it down? We don’t want to bring in the DM (Division Manager).
Me: Oh, okay, what time do you sleep? I’ll try and see what I can do…so sorry bout that.
Them: 11 pm. S’okay, glad to have talked to you because we didn’t know what to say.
Me: Thanks, gnite. (door closes).

E: HAHAHAHA….you’re too loud! Yo, sit in another chair, stop sittin in the one that you’re in..they’re gonna complain…(so I steal Yam’s $2.15 computer chair to solve this problem temporarily)…. Well, I’m guessing they’re new to WCRI. Welcome to cheap student housing. Oh, the guy upstairs just went pee again and hrm, that’s funny. I always hear him pee, a flush..but never the tap water…does he not wash his hands after?! EEEEW.

Update: Think it was good tonight, until Kit came home and I proceeded to tell her what happened. Kit, being like-minded, unconsciously raised her voice, faked DDR, and we laffed SOOO hard…and…DM came up and spoke to Kit (she opened door) about the noise level complaints..mentioned something about being quiet and not even walking with slippers on because we don’t have carpet (excuuuse me?? WCRI didn’t provide us w carpet, so what are we to do?!)…after 11 pm on weeknights (..which leaves…weekends free! muhahah)…but then how on earth does he expect us to walk around!?

Note: w00t! by the grace of God, He passed me on my CS midterm! Some were just stupid careless mistakes I did, like in the definitions section, I changed the right answer to the wrong one and in another question, I forgot to include a command line, which I normally would remember to do. Other answers, especially in the section I counted on losing most of my marks on, must have been divinely inspired. As I looked over my solutions today, I had no idea why I wrote those (correct) answers down or how I derived them.
