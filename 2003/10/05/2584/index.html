stories over coffee

Posted byEmily October 5, 2003 4 Comments on

mid-story:

looking into the restaurant, i see this guy friend of mine and an older woman (w gray hair). i entered the restaurant and joined them in the both, taking the bench opposite them. i soon discovered, to utter shock, that my friend had fallen for her. i couldn’t believe my eyes!

shortly after, we left and headed someplace, where there was a shared bathroom amongst some people. eunice went in and dumped some crap into the shower stall, but as she closed the shower curtain, she heard someone coming in from the other side, so she quickly ran out via the other door. the other girl entered as was about to take a shower, but when she opened the shower curtain, she discovered to her, horror, what someone else had left behind.

eunice, eventually returned to brush her teeth. at first, wanting to be oblivious to knowing what happened, she just ignored the girls questions. she soon felt guilty, and proceeded to explain that she panicked and ran when she heard someone come in, when in fact, she wanted to wash it down the drain to begin with.

as that issue was resolved, we went to a different room and i saw a pet snake (not a garden snake, but one that was round and big). as i went to take a look at it, it lashed out and bit the palm of my hand! normally, the snake was harmless as it was quite tame, but boy, did it ever hurt. i had bite marks where the skin broke. it did that twice before i realised that someone didn’t feed it properly and it was really hungry so it just ate anything within its reach.

i woke up with black marks on my fingers, but i don’t know why.
