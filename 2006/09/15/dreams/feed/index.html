]]> By: j.w https://emdashes.github.io/2006/09/15/dreams/#comment-432 Fri, 15 Sep 2006 22:32:21 +0000 http://558#comment-432 As for dream 1, you’re most likely describing something called “hypnic jerk”. The theory goes that as your muscles relax, right before you sleep, your brain misinterprets this relaxation thinking that you’re falling, so it sends a signal to the rest of your muscles to “jerk”, to keep you from falling. This happens more often to people who are sleep deprived.
