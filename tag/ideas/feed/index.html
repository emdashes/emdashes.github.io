  • You and your friends accept the invite, which gets added to everyone’s individual calendars
  • Assuming you use the same map and calendar app (ie. Google), and you allow this event to be publicly visible or only to those in the same invite in your calendar, when you arrive at the restaurant, the GPS “checks” you in, so it shows everyone you’ve arrived.
