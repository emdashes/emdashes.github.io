02:16:07 +0000 http://her.happyhippos.ca/2010/05/ ]]> all characters mentioned in this post are coincidental and do not represent any factual events in reality…now that I am aware of anyway. this is my dream blog I hadn’t seen them in a very long time, but when I did see Trunkit, I was very saddened to hear that she and Brassman327 had a falling out, and that they ended up becoming divorced. It turned out that ever since I seen them in Ottawa, things got worse and worse. Even sadder was that everyone stopped talking to Brassman327 – at least from the girls’ perspective. Poor him.
I went out, intended to go farther, but because I was running short on time, I decided to sign up for a play. Actually, there were only 8 people who did – 4 girls, and 4 guys. When I bought the pass, which were bought from people in costumes, I didn’t realize that this was for an interactive play. That meant that you were paired with one person of the opposite gender and you actively participated in an improvised play. Each participant was given a story, with lines that the characters need to achieve. For example, the first objective could be: wake up and make breakfast. In the play, you and your partner would say lines and do things on the set to get to that scene before being able to advance to the next objective. In the end, the play would finish when you got to the end of the objectives. So you get to see the story as well as participate in the plot line. And, as much as I don’t like performing publicly (despite me enjoying it very much as a child), it was a really fun experience and a very good idea…]]>
