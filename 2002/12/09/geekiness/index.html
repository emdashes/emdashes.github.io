So anyhow, as I was checking the comments that have been ever collecting on certain entries, I noticed that HaloScan had a new link that said, “Your RSS/XML feed”. First rxn? “What is that!?” So, I did some research, and I thought it was the coolest thing in the world. Hahah, now readers can be automatically notified when I update this page (and read the entry), all without ever having to open their browser. Too bad that not everyone has this nifty RSS/XML feature in their blogs. Because if they did, then Erica/Rumble/TankLau/J.Mo/Jazzy/Wongoz/eBri/D/Jedi-Master/and whomever else’s blog counters won’t be messed and register me 10 times in a day–all because I’m checking for updates! hehe. All one needs is either a small application (e.g. FeedReader) or logged into certain sites to get notifications of updates with the flagged sites of your choice (it’s mainly used for news headlines, etc.). Above all, I am always notified now when comments trickle in as well without having to open my browser. Oh yeah, and I can spy (er, “stalk”?) on other ppl that use HaloScan for their comments too.
