2 More Days…

Posted byEmily December 18, 2002 Leave a comment on 2 More Days…

completely (like, totally escaped my mind) neglected to study for 25% of the exam! (10% out of 40%)–it’s a good thing that my classmate came in 5 mins prior to the exam and said, “so, did you memorize the definitions?”. And, then it occured to me that we were to be tested on key terms in the course as well! *groan* I fired up a quick prayer and quickly scanned her notes and my course notes and prayed that I knew the terms. I dunno how well I did in that section, but I recognised and was vaguely familiar with them but at least God answered my prayer in choosing the question that I hope the prof would ask me to answer (essay portion). Well see how gracious my prof is in that section this time. I got brutally killed in the first midterm. 😐

Some good news for me:
“Will you let me have a clean copy of this journal to put in the Renison Library for future Engl376R journal writers? You’ve really done some lovely thinking about language here. Keep it up!” (prof from Renison College–extension of UW)…wow, I’m honoured! Well, slightly embarrased too. I don’t particularly like showing off my work for future academic scrutiny–even tho’ it’s ironic since I post everything online anyhow. I find that Engl profs at Renison tend to be very good (note Engl, Chinese prof is horrid). The only 2 times I got an ‘A’s in any written work (essays, etc.) was from Renison :P. And, I look fwd to Renison classes in general even though they’re so far, I actually learn in those classes. Shame that I didn’t do that well in my final exam for this particular course. Disappointment to the prof I’m sure. Ah wellz. English grammar is shure doggone a challenge! 😛 My info design course? 83% … could’ve done better, but I’m happy since I learned a lot or at least had practice to do some stuff hands-on and piqued my interest in this area :).

Christmas is around the corner! Now, I can finally “consider” Christmas carols. I really get annoyed at them when I’m in no mood for Christmas or to think about celebrating His berfday!

Yaay, I came home after my exam tonight and was greeted with not 1, but 3 Christmas packages! Being the spoiler that I am, I opened them all in advance because well, I’m leaving soon anyhow… =}

  1. A Christmas card from my “meimei”! Ah yea, remember that girl that I asked you to pray for earlier this term? Well, she’s re-dedicated her life to Christ, but the story hasn’t ended happily ever after yet, and there are still many more battles to face, so continue to pray for her :).
  2. A package from Marcy: the latest MaryMary CD (haha, so my two things I wanted for Christmas has been fulfilled :P), some pix, and my gel ink refill for my Cross Ion pen(s).
  3. JELLY BELLY jellybeans! hahah, that’s the only kind of jellybeans i eat and i buy them when i’m at Sweet Factory, when i decide to spoil myself. How sweet. I’m in awe over them, or maybe over the giver, because I totally didn’t expect anything from him. 🙂 Hrm. Who’s this mystery guy you say? Well, he’s Bonnie’s neighbour. But…he’s also my classmate from CS (gone through ups and downs all with him beginning from CS114). I just realised that I might not see him after tonight because I’m graduating next term *assuming all goes well*, and well, he’s going on co-op in Jan.
I feel bad for not getting anyone anything in return…but, um…*shrugs*, I hope ppl are understanding. Tha’s why my policy is if I give you a gift, don’t give one back because you feel obligated to because that just spoils the fun of giving 🙂

*Ahem*, If I move down Drew’s “society of blogs” over the holidays/weekend, that’s to be expected. Harhar =). I’m going to sauga/TO for the weekend and then home, and well, at home my screen on my laptop is permanently cracked and so i gotta fight to use 1 of the 2 other computers we have…so if I don’t see you before Christmas, have a relaxing one, and don’t forget to wish Jesus a happy belated berfday :P. And to those that will be on co-op term next term, hope you have a good term, and DO come back for my convo! 🙂 (sick, which means change is coming soon. i hate change. i need to do some change management on myself. haha).
