stories over coffee

Posted byEmily January 30, 2004 16 Comments on

Pentel gel pens at a store were selling for $0.57 caught my eye. I paused to look and saw some Winnie-the-Pooh items that I knew my sister would like. As I bent down to examine some things with Melody by my side, I saw someone familiar and noticed that he spoke like Skipper. When I stood upright again, it occured to me that I was in Victoria and that I should call Skipper up to see how he was doing. But before I finished dialing, he already saw me from the entrance of the store and walked over.

Later on, I attended a worship session. The worship leader, after finishing up with songs, proceeded to start off in prayer. We sat down on the floor, gathered in a circle and started to pray for the person on the right of us. Turns out that I ended up praying for Zhakyrnn again.
