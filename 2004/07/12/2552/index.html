stories over coffee

Posted byEmily July 12, 2004 1 Comment on

i had fishies near my tap from the kitchen sink, because they were apparently the easiest pets to maintain, cept someone point out to me that the water needed changing. When i poured them into an udon bowl,  they jumped at me before jumping into it. i made an appointment to see some female doctor on the day before my brother did because she only had time to see someone then and then pondering to see if i should switch family doctors since she seemed to be much better. i ended up seeing a musical/broadway production/play that i thought was titled beauty and the beast with b.chhoa and midway, he got cold so he shared my blanket and i was wondering where carrie was (unless of course, it’s like one of those dreams where i was carrie?).  but, i got up midway through the show and somehow landed backstage, where people would climb down and jump from ladder to ladder. i was stuck on one and almost lost holding but somehow managed to get back to the platform where i heard some of the crew being very upset that they only recently found out one of their cast members had died in vancouver. i quietly asked one if he starred in peter pan or robin hood and when they affirmed it, i told them i was the one who found the body. then as i climbed back out into another section of the theatre, i saw a guy before me who got caught by the security guard for sneaking around and he just pretended that he was lost. so i snuck past him into another section, where i saw the guard coming, so i sat down in a seat, which happened to be behind my sister. and then, it so happened that there was some sort of 50/50 lottery draw in that section and people got up to look at their tickets to see if they won and to buy more. unfortunately, at that point, the exit points to that section were already secured – to keep people from leaving (to prevent scamming of ticket results, etc.) so i didn’t know how to get out. all of  a sudden, gunfire was exchanged and i tried to get behind this wall while trying to see if i could get out while a security guard went at it w its snipers and machine guns. i think i got really tired of waiting, so i just dove through the glass doors, and obviously the crash of the glass sort of alerted everyone. so this guard comes chasing after me while firing along the way, and i’m just running like mad into this giftshop, hopping over counters and products, while a whole bunch of customers and staff were looking on –  with me yelling my head off, “help me! stop this guy!” but then, no one really did anything, or they didn’t know what to do. of course, the shots he fired soon caught up with me and i received multiple gunshot wounds and he ended up catching up to me, but then before he finished me off, i used the rhetoric of pathos in attempt to appeal to his emotional side and asked him about his girlfriend, which somehow made him pause midfire.
